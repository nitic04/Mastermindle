HOW TO PLAY

Guess the colour combination in 10 tries.

Each guess can be any of the 6 given colour options, the combination CANNOT have duplicates.

Press check to check your answer. When this is done, the circles in the square below your guess will change colours based on the correctness of your answer.

If a circle is red, it means one of your choices is the correct colour in the correct position.

If a circle is white, it means that one of your choices is just the correct colour but is not in the correct position.

If a circle does not change colour, it means the colour is incorrect.
